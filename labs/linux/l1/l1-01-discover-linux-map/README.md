## What this lab is about

Before touching files, services or users, you need a mental model of what Linux is.
This lab is **purely exploratory**: you run commands on your own system, observe the output,
and fill in a knowledge map.

No VM needed — your current terminal session is the lab environment.

## What you will do

1. Identify the running kernel version
2. Identify your Linux distribution
3. Explore the most important directories: `/etc`, `/var/log`, `/proc`, `/home`
4. Fill in `notions.md` with your own words
