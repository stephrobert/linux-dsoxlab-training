## What this lab is about

Choosing a distribution is the first real decision on the Linux learning path.
This lab is **purely reflective**: you compare two distribution families on paper,
then justify a choice for three concrete scenarios.

No VM needed — your current terminal is enough.

## What you will do

1. Compare the Debian/Ubuntu family and the RHEL family on key criteria
2. For each of three scenarios, choose a distribution and justify why
3. Fill in `choix-distro.txt` with your answers
